- 2026-07-19 тАФ **Phase 5 done.** `src/lib/charts/polar.ts` (projection) and `all-sky.ts` (model), plus `AllSkyChart.svelte` under `src/components/`, filling the "All-sky view" slot. 48 new tests, 288 total.   - **The track is cut at the rim, not clamped to it** тАФ the opposite of `altitudeToY`'s deliberate clamp on the altitude chart.   - **Interpolating that crossing has to take the short way round the compass.** An object crossing north goes 355┬░ тЖТ 5┬░; straight subtraction sweeps it backwards through the entire sky and puts the rim crossing due *south*. `shortestTurn` wraps the delta into ┬▒180┬░; there is a test pinning it.

A high tree line then reads as a thick wall around the rim, which is what `azimutal.png` shows.
